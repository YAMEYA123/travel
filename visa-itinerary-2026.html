This is a pure leisure tourism trip to the Netherlands, Germany and Austria, covering cultural landmarks, world-class museums, historic city centres, and the world-famous Munich Oktoberfest. No commercial, work-related or academic activities are planned. Both travellers will return to China on 8 October 2026 as scheduled.
